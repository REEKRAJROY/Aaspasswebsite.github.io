About Us

AasPass can change the way people sell, buy and order groceries, it lets you access your city/town like never before. An app that connects & enables the nearest delivery partner who can deliver any daily needed items from any store of your choice in the city and bring them to you. It’s never easy to make purchases or drop off packages when you get busy with work, get stuck in traffic, or you might even end up forgetting about it completely for consumers.It’s never easy to make purchases or maintain records of items in-store when you have many offline customers at your store or shop you might even end up forgetting about it completely for the Storekeeper / Shop keeper.
